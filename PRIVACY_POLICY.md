# Privacy Policy for Scan2PDF

**Last Updated:** [22-Feb-2026]

## Introduction

Scan2PDF ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data We Collect

**App Usage Data (Analytics):**
- Device information (device model, operating system version)
- App version information
- Screen views and navigation patterns
- Feature usage (which features you use)
- Button clicks and user interactions
- Performance metrics (app crashes, errors, processing times)
- File metadata (file types and sizes - **NOT file content**)
- Session data (app opens, session duration)

**Device Identifiers:**
- Device IDs for analytics and advertising purposes

**Authentication Data (Optional):**
- Email address (only if you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In)

### Data We Do NOT Collect

✅ **File Content** - We do NOT collect, transmit, or store the actual content of your files
✅ **Personal Information** - We do NOT collect names, phone numbers, or addresses (except email if you sign in)
✅ **Location Data** - We do NOT collect your location
✅ **Contacts** - We do NOT access your contacts
✅ **Photos/Images** - We only collect metadata (file type, size), not the actual images

## How We Use Information

**Analytics:**
- Understand how users interact with the app
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- Measure app performance

**Advertising:**
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
- Measure ad effectiveness

**Authentication:**
- Securely manage user accounts (if you sign in)

## File Processing

- Files are selected by you through the device's file picker or camera
- Conversion happens **locally on your device**
- PDFs are saved to your device's storage
- **File content is NOT transmitted to external servers**
- We do not retain copies of your files
- Only file metadata (type, size) is collected for analytics

## Permissions

Scan2PDF requests the following permissions:

- **Camera:** To capture documents for scanning
- **File Access:** To read files you select for conversion
- **Storage:** To save converted PDF files to your device

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Firebase Analytics
We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve functionality. Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data as described above.

**Firebase Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Google AdMob
We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and app usage data for ad personalization.

**AdMob Privacy Policy:** https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

### Firebase Authentication
If you choose to sign in, we use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your account. This requires your email address, which is stored securely by Google Firebase.

**Firebase Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Data Sharing

Analytics and advertising data is shared with:
- **Google (Firebase Analytics)** - For app analytics
- **Google (AdMob)** - For advertising

We do NOT sell your data to third parties. Data is shared only with Google in accordance with their privacy policies.

## Data Security

- All data transmission is encrypted in transit
- File processing happens locally on your device
- File content is never transmitted to external servers
- Analytics data is anonymized

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Request data deletion:** Contact us to request deletion of your analytics data
- **Opt-out of analytics:** You can disable analytics (though this may affect app functionality)
- **Access your data:** Request a copy of data we have collected about you
- **File deletion:** Delete your files at any time through your device's file manager

**Note:** Some data may be retained by Google (Firebase, AdMob) in accordance with their privacy policies and data retention policies.

## Children's Privacy

Scan2PDF does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
